Fragrance Free:

Dogs cannot take the harsh chemicals, scents and dyes present in regular pet grooming products. These ingredients may cause rashes & other skin irritations which can be avoided with fragrance free products.

Paraben Free:

Parabens are chemicals that extend the shelf life of products. However, parabens contain carcinogens which cause skin cancer. Parabens are thought to be “stored” in the body and have a cumulative effect posing health risks.
