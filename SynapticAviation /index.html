Why Synaptic Aviation?

Synaptic wasn’t built by tech nerds interested in aviation. It was built by aviation industry experts who happen to also be tech nerds. And now, airlines, airports, ground service providers and MROs depend on Synaptic’s technology to increase their operational visibility, improve efficiency, lower operational costs and enhance safety.

About Synaptic Aviation

Data is meaningless until we can do something with it.

When delivered timely and in the right form, data leads to understanding which can prompt actions resulting in better performance.

For years, information regarding ground operations have been fragmented, inconsistent and unreliable. Synaptic Aviation is helping to eliminate these challenges.

Improve Arrival Readiness

Synaptic monitors each aircraft's arriving stand and sends real-time alerts if crews are absent, obstacles are present or anything else is preventing an on-time arrival at the gate.

Speed Up Turnaround

By accurately recording the start and end times of key turnaround milestones, users can see where they're losing time or at risk of burning unnecessary fuel, and can make adjustments accordingly.

Ensure Safety and Compliance

Whether it's real-time alerts for safety envelope obstruction or tracking compliance for pre-arrival FOD walks, Synaptic is committed to preventing aircraft and property damage, and ground service crews injuries.
