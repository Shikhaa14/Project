Track Mission-Critical Data Points

From arrival to departure, Synaptic Aviation’s AI tracks operational data points such as average turn time, gate utilization, mandatory FOD prevention walks, GPU connection times, and jet bridge status with a log of timestamped events.

Stay Alert with Real-Time Notifications

Synaptic’s AI detects a range of events, such as obstacles in the safety envelope, the presence of guidemen, and service vehicle arrival and departure. Further, it provides ground service teams with real-time alerts if SLA deviations or procedure violations occur, empowering teams to respond immediately.
