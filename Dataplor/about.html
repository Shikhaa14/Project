Uncovering market opportunities in every corner of the globe.

dataplor solves location data challenges to reveal opportunities in established markets and unlock untapped potential in emerging regions.

What we do

dataplor empowers growth by offering comprehensive location intelligence for every public and commercial location in the world, spanning over 200 countries and territories.

Our Story

The data was limited but the potential was vast.

The inception of dataplor was driven by the challenges we noticed in the global location data landscape - it was not only difficult to access but also riddled with inaccuracies. This created significant voids in the market, causing businesses to take speculative risks when venturing into new territories.

Our objective was to bridge these gaps by providing reliable location data to businesses, thereby making the world feel a little less vast and bringing previously overlooked markets into the limelight. We wanted to enable businesses to identify untapped potential and foster closer connections in the global business community.

Our Early Believers

From the very inception of dataplor, our focus has been on addressing tangible customer problems. This commitment has been underpinned by the faith and financial backing from esteemed investors such as ff Venture Capital, Two Lanterns, Navigate Partners, Circadian Ventures, Singtel, Acronym Ventures, Quest Venture Partners, and Space Capital.

Their investments enabled us to find and engage with a group of early adopters, shaping the development of dataplor around their needs. This customer-centric ethos continues to be at the heart of everything we do, guiding us since day one in our mission to solve real-world challenges.
